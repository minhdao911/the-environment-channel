Data Portal

Providing environment-related data to individuals. The Environment Channel will be linked to the measurements and forecasts of precipitation and air quality. The ecosystem can also accommodate other types of data.

Powerful

Providing multi-level data illustration, it delivers a wide range of usage to both individuals and organizations.

visualizing environment around the globe

Observations of the atmosphere, sea and space at over 400 stations around Finland (more to add later), and using remote sensing instruments such as radars and satellites. In addition to weather observations we monitor e.g. air quality, atmosphere components
